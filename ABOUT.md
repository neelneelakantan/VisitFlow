# **About VisitFlow**

VisitFlow is a lightweight, human‑centered workflow system designed to support intentional job‑search habits, reduce cognitive load, and bring structure to a process that is often chaotic, emotional, and unpredictable.

It is not a CRM.  
It is not an automation engine.  
It is not a job‑application tracker.

VisitFlow is a **personal companion** that helps you stay focused, disciplined, and emotionally steady while navigating the modern job market.

---

## **Why VisitFlow Exists**

Modern job search tools optimize for volume, automation, and speed.  
Humans don’t operate that way.

Most job boards:

- overwhelm you with noise  
- bury the signal  
- encourage “spray and pray” behavior  
- provide dashboards that don’t match how humans think  
- offer reminders that are easy to snooze and forget  

VisitFlow takes the opposite approach:

- **Human speed over machine speed**  
- **Intentionality over automation**  
- **Clarity over complexity**  
- **Cognitive unloading over feature bloat**

It gives you a simple, predictable system that keeps important things visible and helps you build habits that compound over time.

---

## **What VisitFlow Does**

VisitFlow provides:

- A clean list of companies you intentionally track  
- A periodic reminder system (daily/weekly/monthly/specific date/none)  
- A structured visit pipeline that turns raw notes into summaries, insights, and narratives  
- A timeline of your progress  
- Free‑flow notes for brain dumps, reflections, and ideas  
- A simple, durable JSON‑based storage model  

Everything is designed to reduce friction and support your mental model — not force you into someone else’s workflow.

---

## **What VisitFlow Does *Not* Do**

VisitFlow intentionally avoids:

- Automated job applications  
- Tracking every job you apply to  
- CRM‑style pipelines  
- Heavy AI integration  
- Complex dashboards  
- Multi‑user features  
- Cloud storage or external dependencies  

These choices keep the system lightweight, private, and emotionally safe.

---

## **Design Philosophy**

VisitFlow is built around a few core principles:

### **1. Human‑centered workflow**
The system supports focus, reflection, and emotional stability — not volume or automation.

### **2. Cognitive unloading**
Your mind should stay clear.  
The system holds the details so you can stay intentional.

### **3. Predictability**
No hidden state.  
No silent overrides.  
No magic.

### **4. Extensibility without complexity**
The architecture is clean, layered, and future‑proof:

- JSON today  
- SQLite tomorrow  
- Agents and NLP later  

### **5. Replaceable components**
Every layer — storage, pipeline, UI — can be swapped without rewriting the system.

---

## **Who This Is For**

VisitFlow is for:

- job seekers who want structure without overwhelm  
- people who prefer intentional workflows over automation  
- anyone who wants a private, local, human‑speed system  
- builders who appreciate clean architecture and clear seams  

It is also a foundation for future exploration: NLP search, browser extensions, background agents, analytics, and more.

---

## **Why It’s Open Source**

VisitFlow is open source because:

- transparency builds trust  
- others may find value in the workflow  
- the architecture can serve as a teaching artifact  
- it demonstrates clean engineering practices  
- it may evolve into a product with community input  

The Apache 2.0 license keeps it permissive while protecting future evolution.
